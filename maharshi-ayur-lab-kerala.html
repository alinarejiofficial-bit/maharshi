Maharshi Ayur Lab Kerala Pvt Ltd

Establisher :1996

Maharshi Ayur Lab began its journey in 1996 and started the manufacturing of Ayurvedic and food products in 1997. In 2003 the company was officially incorporated as a Private Limited Company, and by 2006, it commenced large-scale production at its own well-equipped facility.

Today, Maharshi Ayur Lab Proudly Manufactures :

  • Over 163 Classical Ayurvedic Products
  • More than 43 Proprietary (Patented) Formulations

Our advanced Manufacturing unit is equipped to Produce a wide range of dosage forms and peronal care items,including :

  • Capsules
  • Tablets
  • Granules
  • Candies &Confectionaries
  • Creams & Pastes
  • Soaps and Shampoos
  • Hair Oils & Body Lotions
  • Medicated Oils & Powders
  • Lehyams(Herbal Jams)
  • Fruit Extracts
  • Antioxidant Health Drinks and more

We operate with all the essential Certifications and licenses such as :

  • Drug License(Ayurveda)
  • GMP (Good Manufacturing Practices)
  • fssai (Food Satey and Standards Authority of India)
  • ISO Certification
